ABOUT


Muzen Roll & Boba is your friendly neighborhood source for sushi rolls, bentos, and boba. We bring classic Japanese comfort food with a fun twist to Sherman Oaks.


Our menu is oriented around simple, balanced recipes. Browse our sushi roll menu, which has both your classics and our exciting specialties. Try the Rice Sando, our hot take on the classic katsu sando. Build your own Balanced Bento, with a grain, protein, veggies, and one of our delicious sides. Or treat yourself to a comforting bowl of housemade ramen. Finish it off with a sweet, refreshing boba for a perfect weekday meal or a delightful weekend treat.


Our Story

Our original location, Muzen Sushi, is a full-service family restaurant in the Thousand Oaks area. We opened our second location Muzen Roll and Boba in Sherman Oaks to expand our menu and make it accessible to anyone on-the-go, looking for their sushi or boba fix! Our philosophy for both locations is to put a kind, friendly face on classic Japanese food.

ACCESSIBILITY


Muzen is committed to providing a website that is accessible in terms of both technology and ability. We strive to meet or exceed all applicable standards, including the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0 Level AA (WCAG 2.0 AA). If you experience any difficulty in accessing any part of this website, please feel free to call us at (818) 290-3781 or to email us at mailto:muzenrollandboba@gmail.com. We will work with you to provide the support you seek through an alternate communication method or one that is accessible for you consistent with applicable law (for example, through telephone support). If you do encounter an accessibility issue, please be sure to specify the Web page and we will make all reasonable efforts to make that page accessible.
